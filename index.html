B2B Pharma Client

Finding procurement decision-makers in a locked-down industry

Problem

Needed qualified procurement decision-makers but had no reliable way to identify or reach them.

Strategy

Built a targeted buyer database mapped to the client's ideal customer profile and procurement structure.

Execution

Executed structured LinkedIn outreach with personalised messaging and consistent follow-up.

Result

Generated qualified meetings directly with procurement decision-makers.

Outcome: A repeatable outreach system replacing ad-hoc, unqualified cold contact.
Export Manufacturing Client

Entering a new international market with no existing pipeline

Problem

Wanted to expand into a new export market but had no buyer intelligence or entry plan.

Strategy

Conducted export and market-entry research to identify viable buyer segments and regulatory considerations.

Execution

Ran targeted prospecting and outreach against the identified buyer list.

Result

Established a qualified pipeline of international buyer conversations from a standing start.

Outcome: A documented market-entry playbook the client can reuse for future markets.

FAQ

Common questions

How do you generate leads?+

Through a combination of paid advertising (Google & Meta), targeted outreach (LinkedIn and email), and market research to identify the right buyers — then a structured process to qualify and book meetings with them.

Which industries do you work with?+

Primarily Pharma, Biotech, Healthcare, Manufacturing, Chemicals, Export, SaaS, IT, Consulting and Logistics — industries with longer sales cycles and specific, harder-to-reach buyers.

Do you work internationally?+

Yes. Export market development and international buyer research are core parts of the work, alongside domestic lead generation.

Do you manage ads?+

Yes — Google Ads and Meta Ads, including campaign setup, targeting, creative direction, conversion tracking and ongoing optimisation.

Do you provide market research?+

Yes — competitor research, industry research, buyer research and export/market-entry research, depending on what the engagement needs.

Do you offer appointment setting?+

Yes — meeting scheduling, sales qualification, follow-up and CRM updates, so your sales team only spends time on qualified conversations.
